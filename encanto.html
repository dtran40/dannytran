Arepas con Queso

In Encanto, Julieta Madrigal makes Arepas con Queso, which are the food that heal the ill. This is a beautiful way to highlight how meaningful arepas are in Colombian culture.

Ingredients






*You can get arepa flour online or in the South American section of some supermarkets – you need to make sure you get the right kind, you will find is called Masarepa or Harina de Maiz Refinada.

Recipe

Step 1

Mix together your arepa flour, the warm water, salt and butter in a bowl.

Step 2

Knead all ingredients together until the mixture has a soft consistency – you can mix the ingredients with your hands.

Step 3

Add more water if too dry and more arepa flour if it’s too wet.

Step 4

Add half a cup of grated cheese and mix it into the dough.

Step 5

Once the dough is ready make 5 balls and place them between 2 sheets of baking paper.

Step 6

Roll the balls with a rolling pin so that they are half an inch thick – make sure your arepa is round, cut off extras to shape it round if needed.

Step 7

Get an adult to put a little oil on a pan or griddle and turn the hob onto a medium heat.

Step 8

As soon as the griddle is hot enough you can get your responsible adult to add the Arepas one by one.

Step 9

Cook the arepas on both sides until they are golden and crispy.

Step 10

You can also add some cheese on top while they are cooking.
