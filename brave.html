Scottish Empire Biscuit

In the movie Brave, Merida carries a plate of Scottish Empire Biscuits to the family dinner table. After seeing her three brothers in disgust of their dinner, she sneaks the plate of biscuits under the table to pass to them. A scene later in the movie also shows the three brothers stealing more of those delicious biscuits from the maid.

Cookies

Recipe

Step 1

Preheat oven to 350 degrees.

Step 2

Combine flour, baking powder, salt and set aside.

Step 3

In a stand mixer, cream together softened butter and sugar.

Step 4

Next add milk, followed by one egg at a time, and then vanilla extract.

Step 5

Slowly add in flour mixture until well combined.

Step 6

Drop cookie dough onto a baking sheet and bake 7-9 minutes. Let cookies fully cool down before icing.

Step 7

Combine confectioners sugar with milk and mix until a not too runny consistency is formed. You might need to experiment by adding more milk or icing sugar until the right consistency is achieved.

Step 8

Top iced cookies with glacé cherries.
